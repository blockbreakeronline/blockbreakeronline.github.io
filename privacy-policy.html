Privacy Policy

This statement introduces the Privacy Policy for Block Breaker. The present page describes the way our website handles and keeps your personal data safe.

The gaming experience is improved through basic user information collection which includes usernames along with email addresses. Every piece of sensitive information remains exclusive to Block Breaker and does not get distributed to external organizations.

Data Usage

The data you submit only serves three separate purposes: gameplay improvement, high score tracking and updates about game features functionality.

Security Measures

The platform adopts powerful security protocols for data protection needs alongside its measures for maintaining a safe gaming platform.

In case you have privacy queries get in touch with our support team.
